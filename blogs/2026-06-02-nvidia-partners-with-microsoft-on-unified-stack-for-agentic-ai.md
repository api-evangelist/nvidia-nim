NVIDIA and Microsoft announced a comprehensive partnership to deliver agentic AI across multiple platforms, introducing RTX Spark laptops, DGX Station for Windows, and integration of NVIDIA models into Microsoft Foundry. The collaboration also accelerates data warehouses through GPU technology and advances physical AI capabilities with new AI factory infrastructure. The partnership creates an integrated stack for deploying agentic AI across personal devices, enterprise systems, and cloud infrastructure.
