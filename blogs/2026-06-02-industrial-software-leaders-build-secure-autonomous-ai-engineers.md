NVIDIA unveiled NemoClaw, an open blueprint for creating specialized AI agents that automate industrial engineering workflows for companies like Cadence, Dassault Systemes, and Siemens. These autonomous AI engineers compress weeks of simulation and design work into hours, handling tasks including computer-aided design, meshing, simulation setup, and report generation. Applications span automotive, aerospace, semiconductor, and manufacturing sectors.
