NVIDIA technology partners including Alembic, AWS, Criteo, Higgsfield, KERV.ai, and Taboola demonstrated AI applications in advertising and marketing at the Cannes Lions festival. Highlights include causal AI modeling at enterprise scale, GPU-accelerated bidding systems, agentic AI for marketing workflows, and multimodal content understanding.
