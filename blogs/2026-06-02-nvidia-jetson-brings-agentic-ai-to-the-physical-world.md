NVIDIA announced JetPack 7.2 and NemoClaw support for its Jetson platform, enabling developers to deploy agentic AI systems in robotics, industrial automation, and inspection applications. The release includes OS customization through Yocto project support, CUDA 13 updates, and performance improvements. Companies like Solomon, Advantech, and SandStar are implementing agentic systems in humanoid robots, factory operations, and retail environments.
